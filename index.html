Hair loss treatment varies depending on individual factors such as the underlying cause, genetics, and lifestyle. How to stop hair fall immediately?

Stopping hair fall immediately is challenging. Knowing the root cause of hair loss, managing stress, eating well, and adopting good hair care habits can gradually reduce hair fall in the long term. What is the best treatment to regrow hair naturally?
